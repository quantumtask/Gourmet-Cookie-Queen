Frequently asked questions

How far in advance should I order?

For custom sets, 2–4 weeks ahead is ideal. We can sometimes accommodate rush orders within 7 days — message us with your date and we'll do our best.

What's the minimum order?

Boxes start at 6 cookies. DIY kits, beginner kits, and custom orders have no fixed minimum — just message us with what you need.

How are cookies priced?

Pricing depends on design complexity, shapes, and quantity. Most custom sets land between R50–R100 per cookie. You'll get an exact quote after submitting the design form — no surprises.

Do you ship?

Yes. We ship nationwide using insulated, fragile-safe packaging. Local pickup and delivery are also available — details on the order form.

How long do they stay fresh?

Two weeks at room temperature in their sealed bag. Up to three months frozen. Eat one straight out of the freezer and report back — it's a vibe.

Allergens — please read

We don't cater to allergies or specialty diets. Our cookies contain wheat, dairy, egg, and may contain nuts. Our kitchen handles all common allergens regularly and we can't guarantee any item is allergen-free. If you or your guests have a serious allergy, our cookies are not a safe choice — please don't order.
