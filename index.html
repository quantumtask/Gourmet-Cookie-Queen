Good to know

Frequently asked questions

How far in advance should I order?

For custom sets, 2–4 weeks ahead is ideal. We can sometimes accommodate rush orders within 7 days — message us with your date and we'll do our best.

What's the minimum order?

Custom sets start at one dozen (12 cookies). For weddings, corporate orders, and shipping, the minimum is two dozen.

How are cookies priced?

Pricing depends on design complexity, shapes, and quantity. Most custom sets land between $5–$10 per cookie. You'll get an exact quote after submitting the design form — no surprises.

Do you ship?

Yes. We ship nationwide using insulated, fragile-safe packaging. Local pickup and delivery are also available — details on the order form.

How long do they stay fresh?

Two weeks at room temperature in their sealed bag. Up to three months frozen. Eat one straight out of the freezer and report back — it's a vibe.

Allergens?

Our cookies contain wheat, dairy, and egg. We bake in a kitchen that also handles nuts. Let us know about allergies and we'll guide you on what we can safely accommodate.

Ready to make it unforgettable?

Tell us about your event. We'll come back within one business day with sketches and a quote.
